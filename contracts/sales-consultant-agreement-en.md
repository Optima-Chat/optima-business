## ARTICLE 1: SCOPE OF COOPERATION

1.1 Party B, as an independent sales consultant of Party A, shall be responsible for referring potential clients to Party A and facilitating business collaborations.

1.2 Party A's business scope includes but is not limited to:
- AI software development services
- Big data technology applications and solutions
- Enterprise-level AI Agent system development
- AI technology services including computer vision and natural language processing

1.3 Party B's main responsibilities:
- **Client Development & Referral**: Leverage industry resources and professional background to refer potential clients to Party A, providing client background information and requirement analysis
- **Business Negotiation Support**: Assist Party A in client requirement communication, technical solution presentation, and business term negotiation until formal contract execution
- **Project Implementation Coordination**: Serve as a communication bridge between Party A and clients during project implementation, assisting in resolving client queries, requirement changes, and other issues
- **Delivery Confirmation & Payment Collection**: Assist in promoting project acceptance, delivery confirmation, and payment collection to ensure successful project completion
- **Client Relationship Maintenance**: Maintain client relationships after project delivery, identifying renewal and additional purchase opportunities

**Note**: Party B is required to participate throughout the entire process from client development to project delivery, not merely providing client leads. Commission is settled after actual client payment, reflecting Party B's responsibility for project success.

## ARTICLE 2: COMMISSION STRUCTURE

2.1 **Tiered Commission Rates**:

| Contract Value Range (RMB/HKD) | Commission Rate for This Tier |
|-------------------------------|------------------------------|
| 0 - 500,000 | 15% |
| 500,001 - 1,000,000 | 18% |
| Above 1,000,000 | 20% |

2.2 **Calculation Examples**:
- Contract value 300,000: 300,000 × 15% = 45,000
- Contract value 800,000: 500,000 × 15% + 300,000 × 18% = 75,000 + 54,000 = 129,000
- Contract value 1,500,000: 500,000 × 15% + 500,000 × 18% + 500,000 × 20% = 75,000 + 90,000 + 100,000 = 265,000

2.3 Contract value is based on the total amount specified in the formal contract between Party A and the client (excluding taxes).

2.4 For installment payment projects, commissions shall be settled in batches according to actual payments received from clients.

## ARTICLE 3: CLIENT ATTRIBUTION AND PROTECTION PERIOD

3.1 **Client Attribution Confirmation**:
- Party B must report client information to Party A via email or written form before client referral
- Party A shall confirm within 3 business days whether the client is a first-time contact
- If the client has already been contacted through other channels, Party A has the right to refuse attribution

3.2 **Protection Period**:
- For clients confirmed as attributed to Party B, Party B shall be entitled to commissions on all renewals and additional purchases from such clients for 12 months after the initial contract
- Commission for renewals/additional purchases shall be calculated at 50% of the rates above (i.e., 7.5% for 0-500k, 9% for 500k-1M, 10% for above 1M)

3.3 **Client Loss**:
- If a client is proactively contacted by Party A during the protection period (for projects not referred by Party B), Party B shall still be entitled to renewal commissions
- Attribution rights automatically terminate after the protection period

## ARTICLE 4: PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 Commission Settlement Timeline:
- Party A shall pay the corresponding commission to Party B within **30 business days** after receiving payment from the client
- For installment payments, commissions shall be settled in batches based on each actual payment received

4.2 Payment Method:
- Bank transfer to Party B's designated account
- Party B must provide legitimate invoices (if Party B is a Hong Kong/Mainland company) or signed receipt confirmation (if Party B is an individual)

4.3 Tax Treatment:
- If Party B is an individual, Party A has the right to withhold and remit relevant taxes as required by Hong Kong/Mainland tax laws
- If Party B is a company, each party shall bear its own tax obligations

## ARTICLE 5: CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

5.1 Party B shall maintain confidentiality regarding:
- Party A's technical solutions, source code, and trade secrets
- Party A's pricing strategies and cost structures
- Client information provided by Party A (excluding clients developed by Party B independently)
- The content and commercial terms of this Agreement

5.2 Confidentiality Period:
- During the cooperation period and **3 years** after termination

5.3 Liability for Breach:
- In case of breach of confidentiality obligations, Party B shall compensate Party A for direct economic losses and pay liquidated damages of **RMB/HKD 100,000**

## ARTICLE 6: NON-COMPETE RESTRICTIONS

6.1 During the cooperation period, Party B shall not provide similar sales services to Party A's direct competitors.

6.2 Direct Competitors Definition:
- Companies providing identical or highly similar AI technology services
- AI software development companies competing in the same target market

6.3 Party B may simultaneously provide sales services to technology companies in different industries, provided there is no conflict of interest.

## ARTICLE 7: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 All intellectual property rights in technical solutions, software code, design documents, etc., provided by Party A to clients shall belong to Party A.

7.2 Party A's brand, logo, and promotional materials used by Party B during promotion shall be limited to business promotion purposes only and shall not be used for other commercial purposes.

7.3 Party B shall not make commitments for technical functions or service content beyond Party A's authorized scope.

## ARTICLE 8: TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1 Term of this Agreement: **12 months** from the date of execution, renewable upon expiration.

8.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days** written notice to the other party.

8.3 Upon termination:
- Commission rights for clients attributed during the protection period remain valid
- Attribution rights for incomplete transactions shall lapse
- Confidentiality obligations continue to be effective

8.4 Party A has the right to terminate this Agreement immediately in the following circumstances:
- Party B seriously violates confidentiality obligations or non-compete restrictions
- Party B makes false commitments to clients, damaging Party A's reputation
- Party B fails to refer any valid client leads for 6 consecutive months

## ARTICLE 9: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the **Hong Kong Special Administrative Region**.

9.2 The parties shall resolve disputes through friendly negotiation. If negotiation fails, the dispute shall be submitted to the **Hong Kong International Arbitration Centre** for arbitration.

9.3 Arbitration costs shall be borne by the losing party.

## ARTICLE 10: MISCELLANEOUS

10.1 This Agreement is made in duplicate, with each party holding one copy, both having equal legal effect.

10.2 For matters not covered in this Agreement, the parties may execute supplemental agreements, which shall have the same legal effect as this Agreement.

10.3 This Agreement shall become effective upon signature and seal by both parties.
